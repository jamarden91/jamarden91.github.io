The following is a step by step guide on how to corectly set up a brand new aquarium

So you have decided to get yourself an aquarium, befor you go out and buy it there are a few thing you might want to consider. First and formost what is your budget, setting up a brand new fish tank isnt cheep by any means so you probably want to go price some it before you go and buy 50 gallon tank. Secondly is where are you going to put it and what are you going to put it on tank starnds can cost just as much if not more than the tank its self. Finaly how much time do you want to put into it the bigger the tank the more offten you will have to clean it and the longer it will take you to clean it.

Step 1

Buying the tank and equipment
There are a lot more thing you need than you would think to maintianing a clean and healthy fish tank. To set you off on the right track hear are a few things you will need to get started.

    • Fish tank
      Substraight (gravle, sand, dirt, .ect)
      Gravle cacume
      Filter (make shure it is the corect size)
      Declore
      Decorations (optional but perfered)
      Heater

    • Step 2

    Thoughly wash substraight and decorations DO NOT use soap or detergents because they are highly toxic to the fish. Than add your substraight, decoratios and equipment to the tank; Idealy you should arange decore to hide equipment like bubblers, heaters, and the fillter.

    Step 3

    Fiil the tankt pouring water on to a saucer sitting on the gravle to not desturbe the substraight till the water is level with the trim of the tank. Now its time to turn on you filter, heater and any other equipment you might have. Heater should be set to approximitly 75 degrees farinhight and fill the filter with water before turning it on so not to burn out the pump. It is ideal to let the tank set for tow or three days before adding fish so that it may start its cycle and grow the healthy bactira need for your fish to survive.

    Step4

    Finaly you get to add fish, it is ideal to do research on what kind of fish you want to get if they will make good tank mates. Preferibly you would want to get all peacefull and some simagresiv fish, or all aggresive fish. If you are not shure hears some INFO to help you decide. When adding your fish try not to add pet store water due to the high bactira levels in the water. It is also a good idea to not add all of you fish at once to not over load you eco system.
