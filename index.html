Betta fish care 101

Genral facts

Betta fish, aka Siamese fighting fish, are among the most popular home aquarium pets, second only to goldfish. With the wide variety of stunning betta colors and shapes to choose from it is no supprise that these fish are so popular, yet they where not always this way. The Wild-bettas of Asia are much duller colered and there fins are much smaller.

Just like every other fish they clean water to survive so the water in there bowls should be changed partoly atleast once a week and a full water change once every two weeks. when doing water changes it is best if you can get declorinated water although not onehundered percent nesicary it dose reduce chances of sickness with your fish. When feeding your Betta fish you shoud feed him in small amounts twice a day every day, if he isnt eating all of the food it means you are probably over feeding withch can be cured by smaller food quantities at feeding time.
